Instructions

  1. Land on any planet and get a point of reference. This can be your starship, a save beacon, or navigational marker.
  2. Record your current coordinates in the first set of input fields labeled "lat" and "long." Remember to enter as positive or negative numbers with decimals.
  3. Walk or drive at least 1000u from your reference point and record your new position in the second set.
  4. Use your visor to find the distance between your current position and the reference point. Record this in the "Distance" field.
    5. TIP: press Enter to move to the next input field for speedy use :)

NOTE: Distance shown when you point at your marker switches from units to minutes somewhere between 1100u and 1200u.

There is also a video guide in case you need more detailed instructions :)

3-Star Deposits

3-star deposits are a type of resouce found on every planet.
Here are the different types of deposits:

Curious Deposit:
• These drop large amounts of Runaway Mould, which refines 5:1 into nanites.
• Requires the advanced mining laser tech installed.
• It's recommend that you build walls around them as they will roll away after being hit.
• Having the Optical Drill installed increases the yield.
• This is usually what people are looking for.

Metal Fingers:
• Will drop large amounts of either Uranium or Gold.
• Requires the advanced mining laser multitool tech installed.

Venom Sac:
• Drops Sac Venom
• Getting too close will damage you and picking them up will alert sentinels to level 3 (quad spawns).
• Also obtainable via the Venom Urchin plant
• Sac Venom sells for 60,800 units

Deposit Leylines

Leylines are lines of longitude (vertical lines that go from north to south) that will spawn 3 star deposits.

If you find a leyline - either by yourself or by using this tool - all you have to do is to go straight North or South to find deposits.

Leylines found using this calculator are valid between 45°N and 45°S. That doesn't mean there will be no deposits. It means that leylines aren't fully certain outside of that range.
